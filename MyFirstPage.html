How to Make PB & J Sandwich

  1. Grab two slices of bread
  2. Grab a jar of peanut butter
  3. Grab a jar of jelly
  4. Use a knife or spoon to spread peanut butter onto first slice of bread
  5. Use a knife or spoon to spread jelly onto second slice of bread
  6. Place both pieces of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly sides touching each other
